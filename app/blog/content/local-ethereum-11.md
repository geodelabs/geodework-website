### Asia-Pacific

- **India:** The Ethereum Foundation selected [Mumbai to host Devcon 8](https://x.com/EFDevcon/status/1992324373500567800?s=20), recognizing India as the one of the world's fastest-growing developer hub. India onboarded [17% of all new crypto developers](https://www.developerreport.com/reports/devs/2024?s=established-developers-contribute-the-most-code-70-of-code-commits-come-from-established-developers) globally, the highest of any country. With over [21.9 million developers](https://github.blog/news-insights/octoverse/octoverse-a-new-developer-joins-github-every-second-as-ai-leads-typescript-to-1/) on GitHub and leading global crypto adoption for the [second consecutive year](https://www.chainalysis.com/blog/asia-pacific-crypto-adoption-2025/), India's grassroots Ethereum communities (Devfolio, Polygon, EthMumbai) have created a thriving Web3 ecosystem. India also plans to launch¬†[**ARC**](https://x.com/coinbureau/status/1991419278931722373?s=20), a rupee-backed stablecoin built by Polygon, in Q1 2026 to operate within the Reserve Bank of India's CBDC framework.
- **Japan**: Japan's Financial Services Agency is pushing legislation to slash crypto profit taxes from [55% to 20%](https://asia.nikkei.com/spotlight/cryptocurrencies/japan-moves-to-apply-flat-20-tax-on-crypto-profits) (matching stock trading rates). Sony Bank also plans a [USD-pegged stablecoin](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2025/12/01/sony-bank-could-issue-usd-stablecoin-in-us-next-year-nikkei) for gaming and anime by fiscal 2026. Japan's three megabanks (MUFG, SMFG, Mizuho) [received approval](https://www.coindesk.com/policy/2025/11/07/japan-regulator-to-support-country-s-3-largest-banks-in-stablecoin-issuance) for yen-stablecoin pilots, with JPYC becoming the country's first regulated yen-backed stablecoin.
- **South Korea**: The ruling party demands passage of the Digital Asset Basic Law by January 2026, establishing a "Korean-style stablecoin" with banks holding 51%+ equity. KakaoBank is developing a [won-pegged stablecoin](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/kakaobank-pushes-forward-korean-won-113853916.html). [Unpacking the state of Ethereum ecosystem in South Korea by Obol](https://app-na1.hubspotdocuments.com/documents/50060267/view/1555577921?accessId=96d141) and DSRV.
- **China**: The central bank reaffirmed its crypto ban following multi-agency meetings, explicitly flagging stablecoin risks. Despite restrictions, blockchain infrastructure advances: a letter of credit system launched using dual-layer "[Hangmao Chain + GFIX](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/_KHoapye_4MjY3EpVK7a0Q)" architecture with major banks (ICBC, Bank of China, Shanghai Pudong Development Bank), covering full on-chain trade finance services. The [National Development and Reform Commission](https://zfxxgk.ndrc.gov.cn/web/iteminfo.jsp?id=20560) also released guidelines [promoting blockchain](https://wallstreetcn.com/livenews/3002060) for logistics digitalization.
- **Hong Kong**: Hong Kong prices [world‚Äôs first government bonds](https://www.scmp.com/business/banking-finance/article/3332391/hong-kong-prices-worlds-first-government-bonds-tokenised-settlement) with tokenised settlement. [The Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA)](https://www.hkma.gov.hk/eng/news-and-media/press-releases/2025/11/20251113-3/) also announces the new phase of Project Ensemble to support real-value transactions in tokenised deposits and digital assets. Regarding Tokenization/RWA, Hong Kong government is actively exploring applications in international shipping, corporate treasury management, and other [real use cases](https://www.21jingji.com/article/20251118/herald/8af30ad3d64d4e37a58702a087191ba9.html).
- **Singapore**: MAS announced a 2026 pilot for [tokenized government bills settled using wholesale CBDC](https://www.theblock.co/post/378698/singapore-trial-tokenized-bills-cbdc), and bring in laws to regulate stablecoins as it presses forward with plans to build a scalable and secure tokenised financial ecosystem.
- **Philippines**: Everything you need to learn about [The Philippines Ethereum Ecosystem Overview](https://localethereum.substack.com/p/the-philippines-ethereum-ecosystem).
- **United Arab Emirates**: New [Ethereum Community Hub](https://x.com/EFetheverywhere/status/1997301506236625155?s=20) in Dubai at [Hadron Founders Club](https://x.com/Hadronfc).
